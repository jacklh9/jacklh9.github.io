About

Hello. My name is Jack, and I’m a technologist. I am interested in software development on a variety of platforms in support of process automation, reporting, monitoring and alerting, dashboards, etcetera. I have over two decades of hands-on experience in I.T. that began in pc repair and tech support and continued in my career in systems and application administration and back-end programming. I’ve touched many technologies over the years, in various projects and situations. Each one had a unique story behind them, some simple and mundane solutions, and others more noteworthy. As a result, my experiences have left me platform and language agnostic. I recognize that different systems have their strengths in certain circumtances and to rule out any, out of a platform religious zeal is shortsided and sophomoric. I believe in the right tool for the right job and setting, knowing there’s more than one way to skin a cat. Sometimes there's a need to use widespread, well-understood, industry-standard vendor proprietary software; othertimes, it's wise to make use of the plethora of Open-Source software and APIs made available to the world. As such, I try to delve into as many technologies as possible, from Windows to Linux to OSX, and Android to iOS. Variety, after-all, is the spice of life.

Yours truly,

Jack Hamilton
